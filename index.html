Welcome to my homepage! I am an Architect and an independent researcher in the field of Social Architecture and Community Engagement.
I recieved My Bachelor's degree in Architecture from the Art University of Isfahan, then I pursued my Master's degree in Architecture at Shahid Beheshti University. During the Master's program, I conducted a research as the fulfillment of my Master's dissertation project titled "the Right to the City; a Rethinking of the Role of Architects in Processes of Improving Informal Settlements". Following my previous research project, I am currently working as the lead author on a book titled "the Role of Architects in Informal Settlements-Claiming the Right to the City". Additionally, I am working as a research assistant on participatory actions in the context of Iran's informal settlements at Urban Regeneration Corporation of Iran, and I am collaborating with Tehran Urban Innovation Center (TUIC) as a researcher/designer. Previously, I worked as an architectural designer at Ayeneh Office for about two years, where I was in charge of several housing projects.
