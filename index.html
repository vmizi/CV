Welcome to my homepage! I am an Architect and an independent researcher in the field of Digital Architecture and Computational Design.
I recieved My Bachelor's degree in Architecture from the Azad University of Isfahan, then I pursued my Master's degree in Architecture at Shahid Beheshti University. During the Master's program, I conducted a research as the fulfillment of my Master's dissertation project titled "Designing a Residential Complex Emphesizing on Optimizing the Acquisition of Natural Light". Following my previous research project, I worked as an architectural designer at VIN Architects Office for about two years, where I was responsible for energy performance analysis and also creating parametric geometry and patterns
